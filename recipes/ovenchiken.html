OVEN FRIED CHICKEN

I love fried chicken, but it takes so long and is such a big mess. I came up with this oven-fried version, and have been making it ever since! Everyone I have given the recipe to has loved it as well.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Flour
  • Bread Crumbs
  • Eggs
  • Salt and pepper
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Step 1: Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Step 2: Place flour in a shallow plate or bowl and season with salt and pepper to taste. Put bread crumbs in another shallow plate or bowl and beat eggs in another bowl.
  3. Step 3: Dredge chicken piece by piece in the flour, then the egg, then the bread crumbs, until all pieces are coated.
  4. Step 4: Pour oil into a large baking dish; add the breaded chicken thighs and sprinkle with paprika to taste.
  5. Step 5: Bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes, then turn pieces over and bake for another 30 minutes. Remove from the oven and drain on paper towels.
  6. Step 6: Serve hot and enjoy!

Takes long time to cook but it's 100% worth it.
